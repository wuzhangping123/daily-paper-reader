## 摘要
提出并分析了一种在p阶张量的局部Lipschitz光滑性下的新型快速自适应正则化方法。对于非凸问题，该方法在达到一阶ε-驻点时实现了最优的O(|log(ε)|ε^{-(p+1)/p})复杂度；在凸情况下，该方法以O(|log(ε)|ε^{-1/p})次迭代将最优性差距降至ε以下，从而在忽略对数项的情况下，与全局Lipschitz光滑性下标准张量方法的复杂度界相匹配。所提出的算法遵循标准张量方法的路线，并采用适当选择的正则化及适当修改。针对一些非凸回归问题，我们与标准自适应三次正则化进行了初步数值实验和比较，展示了所提方法的一些潜力。

## Abstract
A new, fast adaptive regularization methods is proposed and analyzed under local Lipschitz smoothness of the $p$-th order tensor. For nonconvex problems, it achieves the optimal $\mathcal{O}\!\left(|\log(ε)|ε^{-(p+1)/p}\right)$ complexity to obtain first-order $ε$-stationary points and in the convex case, it yields $\mathcal{O}\!\left(|\log(ε)|ε^{-1/p}\right)$ iterations to drive the optimality gap below $ε$, thus matching the complexity bounds of standard tensor methods under global Lipschitz smoothness yp to logarithmic terms. The proposed algorithm follows the line of standard tensor methods with an appropriately chosen regularization and suitable modifications. Initial numerical experiments and comparisons for some nonconvex regression problems are made with the standard adaptive cubic regularization where we showcase some potential of the proposed method.
