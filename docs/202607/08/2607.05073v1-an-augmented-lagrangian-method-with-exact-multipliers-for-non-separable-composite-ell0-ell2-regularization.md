## 摘要
本文研究了一种非可分复合ℓ0-ℓ2正则化模型，该模型同时针对逆问题施加稀疏性和平滑性。ℓ0范数引入了固有的非凸性和非光滑性，而线性变换进一步引入了不可分性，使得该问题的求解在计算上具有挑战性。现有的不精确增广拉格朗日方法存在计算复杂度高和收敛不稳定的问题。为克服这些困难，我们开发了两种新颖的具有精确乘子的增广拉格朗日算法，分别针对全行秩情况和一般矩阵情况设计，其中所有子问题均通过闭式解实现全局优化。此外，我们证明了当变换矩阵为全行秩时，所提方法具有线性收敛性。在一般设置下，生成序列的所有聚点均为原问题的KKT点。在合成数据、趋势滤波和图像平滑上的数值实验表明，所提方法在效率和精度上均优于现有方法，证实了我们的理论分析。

## Abstract
This paper studies a non-separable composite $\ell_0$-$\ell_2$ regularization model that simultaneously enforces sparsity and smoothness for inverse problems. The $\ell_0$ norm induces inherent nonconvexity and nonsmoothness, while linear transformations further introduce nonseparability, making the problem computationally challenging to solve. The existing inexact augmented Lagrangian method suffers from high computational complexity and unstable convergence. To overcome these difficulties, we develop two novel augmented Lagrangian algorithms with exact multipliers, designed respectively for the full row-rank case and the general matrix case, where all subproblems are globally optimized via closed-form solutions. Furthermore, we prove linear convergence of the proposed method when the transformation matrix is full row rank. In the general setting, all accumulation points of the generated sequence are KKT points for the original problem. Numerical experiments on synthetic data, trend filtering, and image smoothing demonstrate the superior efficiency and accuracy of the proposed methods over the existing method, confirming our theoretical analysis.
